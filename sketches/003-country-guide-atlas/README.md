## Variant: Country guide atlas

### Design stance
Editorial country pages make international planning quickly navigable.

### Key choices
- Layout: country tabs, per-country hero guide, region/resort cards, comparison table.
- Typography/color: premium dark atlas with travel-guide hierarchy.
- Interaction: country tab switches full country page context and preserves hash anchors.
- Planning data: each country page shows gateway, car need, lift/day, 7-night target, resort quick metrics, tips, cost ranges, and source freshness labels.

### Trade-offs
- Strong at: country navigation and international context.
- Weak at: less optimized for urgent fare-sale calculations.

### Best for
Users asking “what would Japan/Canada/Austria look like?” before choosing a region.
